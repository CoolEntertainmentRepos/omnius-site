Features

Everything Your Users Expect

A complete streaming experience out of the box. No compromises on UX, no features missing. Built for Android TV and macOS with a production-ready admin panel and API backend.

TV-Optimized UI

Beautiful, familiar interface built with Jetpack Compose for TV. Smooth animations, hero banners, and card-based browsing.

D-pad Navigation

Full remote control support for Android TV. Navigate the entire app effortlessly with a standard TV remote.

In-App Player

Built-in ExoPlayer with HLS, DASH, and torrent streaming. Full playback controls, seek, and quality selection.

Subtitles

Auto-load subtitles in 60+ languages. SubDL integration with language preferences and auto-selection.

Continue Watching

Resume where you left off. Automatic progress tracking with a dedicated home screen row for partially-watched content.

Favorites

Users can save their favorite movies and series for quick access. Persistent across app restarts.

Search & Browse

Find content by title, genre, or rating. Browse by category with lazy-loaded grids and infinite scroll.

Live TV

Thousands of live channels organized by country and category. Built-in EPG-style browsing with instant playback.

Series & Episodes

Full TV series support with seasons, episodes, and per-episode progress tracking. Watched indicators and resume points.

P2P Streaming

Built-in torrent engine (libtorrent4j) for peer-to-peer content delivery. Reduces server bandwidth costs.

Configurable Server

Point the app to your own API backend. Configurable server URL means you control the content catalog entirely.

Watch History

Automatic tracking of everything watched. Progress bars on thumbnails, watched indicators, and smart resume points.
